1. How Trading Works

Forex trading is like exchanging money with the aim of making profit. You trade currency pairs, such as EUR/USD or GBP/USD, based on which one you believe will get stronger. Traders buy low and sell high, using charts and news to make decisions.

3. Facts and Myths About Trading

• You can see live market prices and trade instantly.
• You can trade anytime; markets are active 24/7.
• You can start with small capital.
• It is possible to lose money fast.
• Trading requires discipline and patience.
• Borrowing too much money (leverage) increases risk.

4. What Helps You Win

• Learn to read charts (technical analysis).
• Follow global events and news (fundamental analysis).
• Do not risk money you cannot afford to lose.
• Stay calm and follow your trading plan.

In short: Trading is not a get‑rich‑quick scheme. It is a skill that improves with practice and patience.
